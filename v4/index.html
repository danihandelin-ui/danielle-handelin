About

The person
behind the systems.

I've spent my career at the intersection of people and process, the place where a good system either holds things together or quietly falls apart. My background runs through higher education, nonprofits, hospitality, and operations, and the through-line in all of it is the same: I find what's broken and I fix it.

At Montana State, I built a Jira advising system that leadership formally adopted across the department. I have led CRM migration work while keeping teams supported and aligned, and I have coordinated complex events that required steady communication across stakeholders. I do my best work when the stakes are real and the brief is complicated.

Outside of work, I run trails, host a women's book club, and cook everything from scratch. At home, I also have a small farm of personality with 2 dogs and 4 cats. I believe in warmth without fluff, precision without coldness, and follow-through that feels like relief.
